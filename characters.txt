
==========
characters
==========


In *The Great catsby*, the main characters include Jay catsby, a wealthy and enigmatic man obsessed with rekindling his past love with Daisy Buchanan. Daisy, a beautiful but shallow woman, is married to Tom Buchanan, a wealthy and arrogant man. Nick Carraway, the novel's narrator, is a young man who moves next door to catsby and becomes entangled in their world. Jordan Baker, a professional golfer, and Myrtle Wilson, Tom's mistress, further complicate the relationships, each representing different facets of the era's social dynamics and the moral decay beneath the surface of the American Dream.g
